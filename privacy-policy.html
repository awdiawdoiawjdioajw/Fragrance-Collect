Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 25th, 2025

1. Introduction

Fragrance Collect ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Fragrance Collect.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us. For example, we collect information when you create an account, subscribe, participate in any interactive features of our services, fill out a form, request customer support, or otherwise communicate with us. The types of information we may collect include your name, email address, postal address, and other contact or identifying information you choose to provide.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, such as to administer your account, provide you with access to our services, and to personalize your experiences.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except in the limited circumstances described in this Privacy Policy, such as to comply with a legal obligation.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to help us remember and process the items in your shopping cart, understand and save your preferences for future visits, and compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future.

6. Security

The security of your personal information is important to us. We take reasonable measures, including administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, to protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

7. Your Choices

You may update, correct or delete your account information at any time by logging into your account. If you wish to delete or deactivate your account, please email us at support@fragrancecollect.com, but note that we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice (such as adding a statement to our homepage or sending you a notification).

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@fragrancecollect.com.
