Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right fragrance?

Our fragrance experts can help you find your perfect scent based on your preferences, skin type, and occasion. Contact us for a personalized consultation.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unopened and unused fragrances. Contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping times and costs vary by location. Contact us for specific details.

Can I get samples before purchasing?

We offer sample sets for many of our fragrances. Contact us to inquire about available samples for your preferred scents.
