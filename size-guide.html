Fragrance Size Guide

Understanding fragrance sizes and choosing the perfect amount for your needs

Fragrance Size Categories

Sample Size

1-5 ml

Perfect for testing new fragrances before committing to a full bottle.

  • 1-3 applications
  • Travel-friendly
  • Cost-effective testing

Mini Size

10-15 ml

Ideal for travel and occasional use. Great for limited edition fragrances.

  • 10-15 applications
  • TSA compliant
  • Compact storage
Most Popular

Standard Size

50-100 ml

The most common size for daily wear. Perfect balance of value and longevity.

  • 50-100 applications
  • 6-12 months of use
  • Best value per ml

Large Size

125-200 ml

For fragrance enthusiasts who want extended use of their favorite scents.

  • 125-200 applications
  • 1-2 years of use
  • Premium presentation

Usage Guidelines

Daily Wear

For everyday use, 1-2 sprays are typically sufficient. A 50ml bottle will last approximately 6-8 months with daily use.

Tip: Apply to pulse points for longer-lasting fragrance.

Occasional Wear

For special occasions or weekend wear, you might use 2-3 sprays. A 50ml bottle can last 1-2 years with occasional use.

Tip: Layer with matching body products for enhanced longevity.

Travel Use

For travel, consider 10-15ml sizes which are TSA compliant and perfect for short trips. Mini sizes are ideal for carry-on luggage.

Tip: Store in a cool, dry place to preserve fragrance integrity.

Gift Giving

Standard sizes (50-100ml) are perfect for gifts. They offer good value and are universally appreciated by fragrance lovers.

Tip: Consider the recipient's lifestyle when choosing size.
